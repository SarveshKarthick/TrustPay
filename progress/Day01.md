\## 💡 Key Decisions



Today wasn't about writing code.



It was about making the decisions that every line of code will depend on later.



Some important decisions made today:



\- TrustPay will be a \*\*mobile-first application\*\*.

\- Existing UPI apps will be integrated instead of replaced.

\- Payments will remain locked inside an escrow workflow until milestones are approved.

\- Documentation-first development will be followed throughout the project.



\---



\## 📚 Lessons Learned



A successful product starts long before the first feature is implemented.



Planning the architecture, defining the problem clearly, and documenting requirements reduces technical debt and avoids unnecessary redesigns later in development.
